=====================================================
Marketing Experience Management (MxM) Prerequisites
=====================================================


Prerequisites
==============

* Adobe InDesign CC must have been installed in your system.
* Desktop Publisher (DTP) client must have been installed and integrated with Adobe InDesign CC.
* You must have valid credentials to access the Contentserv system.
* At least one product template must exist in the system.
* At least one document template must exist in the system.
* At least one publication template must exist in the system, which may contain a single document template or multiple document templates. It may contain a single document template multiple times.
