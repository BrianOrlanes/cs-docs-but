Promotional Bundle¶

A bundle is a set of multiple Marketing Products that are put together to form a bundle. It offers several products for sale as one combined product. The bundle may contain complementary products and is used for promotion and marketing of your business products. You can sell a set of products at a lower price compared to the total cost of all the included items. Bundling allows consumers to benefit from a single, value-oriented purchase of complementary offerings.

Pursuing a bundle pricing strategy allows the supplier or retailer to increase the profit by giving customers a discount when buying the bundled products. Note that the customer cannot choose to buy only some of the items from the bundle. For example, a retailer offers a promotional bundle for women, and this bundle consists of a tunic wear, a bag, and a pair of shoes. A customer needs to purchase this bundle as a whole. A retailer can sell this bundle at a lower cost compared to the cost of buying the items separately.


Purpose:
  • The purpose of this document is to show you how to make a promotional bundle in the system. You will be guided through the different stages that are involved in the configuration of a promotional bundle.
Target Audience:
  • Administrators
  • Marketing Professionals
  • Analysts
Prerequisites:
  • You must have valid credentials to access Contentserv PXP system.
  • More than one Marketing Products must exist in the system.
Key Terms:
  • Marketing Product: the Marketing Product represents the marketing and promotional sales aspect of MDM product. It is virtually composed of MDM article along with the marketing and sales information.


How-to: Use a Promotional Bundle¶

Configure a Promotional Bundle from the Administration Side¶

  • Log in and go to Administration.

  • Navigate to Data Model → Classes → Marketing Content → Marketing Bundle menu.

  • Click on the “+” icon to create the marketing bundle.

  • On the pop-up window, enter the Name and Code. Enable the slider if you want to create a nature class. Select Promotional Bundle from Type then click on the Create button.

  • Go to Explore to view the Dashboard.

Create a Promotional Bundle¶

  • Click on the user profile and select MxM from the drop-down list.

  • From the left pane, click on Bundles.

  • Click on the Create drop-down menu and select the created Marketing Bundle class to make a bundle out of Marketing Products.

  • New bundle is created and you will be redirected to the content open view for a bundle.

  • In the Marketing Products section under the Overview tab, add products to make a promotional bundle.

  • Click on the Add Product(s) icon.

  • Select the Marketing Products that you want to add to the bundle.

  • Click on the Add icon.

  • Click on the Back icon to go to the bundle content open view screen.

  • In the Overview tab, view the Marketing Products that have been added to the Marketing Products section.

  • From the content open view, click on the Edit icon and enter a name for the bundle.

  • Click on the Relationships tab and click on the “+” icon from the Images section to add an image to the bundle.

  • Select an existing image or add a new image. Click on the Create drop-down menu and select Image.

  • In the image class open view, click on the Add Image icon to upload an image.

  • Click on the Save button.

  • Go back to the content open view for a bundle to view the image added to the bundle.

  • View the created marketing bundle in the Bundles menu.
